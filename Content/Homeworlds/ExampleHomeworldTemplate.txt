This file demonstrates how to configure a Homeworld Data Asset in the Unreal Engine editor.

## Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Create a new Homeworld Data Asset:

## Example 1: Core World Homeworld (Terra Prime)

**Homeworld Info:**
- Homeworld Name: "Terra Prime"
- Homeworld ID: TerraPrime
- Description: "A prosperous core world colony loyal to advanced civilizations. 
  Citizens enjoy high technology and strong military protection."

**Faction Relations:**
  Entry [0]:
    - Faction: DA_Faction_SolarisUnion (select from dropdown)
    - Starting Reputation: 75

  Entry [1]:

## Reputation Scale Reference:

-100 to -50: Hostile (faction attacks on sight)
-49 to -25:  Very Unfriendly (closed borders, no trade)
-24 to -1:   Unfriendly (limited interaction)
0 to 24:     Neutral (standard diplomacy)
25 to 49:    Friendly (trade bonuses)
50 to 74:    Very Friendly (alliance opportunities)
75 to 100:   Allied (maximum cooperation)

---

## Important Notes:

1. Each homeworld should have 5-10 faction relationship entries
2. Not all factions need entries (undefined = neutral/0 reputation)
3. Ensure Faction references point to valid FactionDataAsset objects
4. Homeworld IDs must be unique across all homeworlds
5. Balance friendly vs hostile factions for gameplay variety
