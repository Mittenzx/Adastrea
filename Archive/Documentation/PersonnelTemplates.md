# Personnel Role Templates and Descriptions

## Overview

This document provides detailed descriptions of all available personnel roles, their typical stat ranges, recommended traits, and example configurations. Use these as guidelines when creating personnel for your game.

## Template Location

YAML templates for common roles are available in:
```
Assets/PersonnelTemplates/
```

Available templates:
- `Captain.yaml`
- `Pilot.yaml`
- `Gunner.yaml`
- `Engineer.yaml`
- `MedicalOfficer.yaml`
- `StationManager.yaml`
- `CrewMember_Generic.yaml`

## Role Categories

### Command Roles

#### Captain
**Description**: Commander of a ship or fleet vessel. Responsible for all crew and ship operations.

**Typical Stats:**
- Overall Skill Level: 8-10
- Age: 35-60
- Salary: 12,000-20,000 credits/month

**Key Skills:**
- Leadership (9-10)
- Tactical (7-9)
- Navigation (6-8)
- Diplomacy (6-8)

**Common Traits:**
- Natural Leader
- Combat Veteran

## Role Selection Guide

When choosing a role for your personnel:

1. **Consider the setting**: Ships need pilots and engineers; stations need managers and staff
2. **Balance the crew**: Don't create all specialists; include general crew
3. **Match skills to role**: Ensure key skills align with the role's responsibilities
4. **Think about progression**: Lower roles can advance to higher ones over time
5. **Create variety**: Mix experience levels and backgrounds for interesting dynamics
